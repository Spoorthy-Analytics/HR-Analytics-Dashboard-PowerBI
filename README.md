# HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides HR professionals and business leaders with insights into employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and education background. The dashboard helps identify patterns affecting employee retention and supports data-driven HR decisions.
---
## Dashboard Highlights
### Key KPIs
- Total Employees: 1,470
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Active Employees: 1,233
- Average Age: 37 Years

- Employee Distribution by Age Group

- Job Satisfaction Rating by Job Role

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. What is the overall attrition rate?
2. Which department has the highest attrition?
3. Which education fields experience the highest employee turnover?
4. How does attrition vary across age groups?
5. What are the job satisfaction levels across different job roles?
6. What workforce demographics influence employee retention?
